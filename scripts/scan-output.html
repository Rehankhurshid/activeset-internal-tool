FAQ’s

Frequently Asked Questions

(01)
What exactly is included in Webflow maintenance?

Our maintenance covers everything needed to keep your Webflow site running smoothly: content updates, performance optimization, security updates, bug fixes, SEO maintenance, and regular backups. We handle both routine maintenance and urgent issues as they arise.

(02)
How quickly do you respond to maintenance requests?

Response times depend on your plan. Essential plan clients get responses within 24 hours, Growth plan clients within 12 hours, and Enterprise clients get same-day response for urgent issues. We prioritize based on business impact.

(03)
Can you handle complex Webflow customizations during maintenance?

Yes. Our team includes experienced Webflow developers who can handle advanced customizations, complex CMS implementations, and custom code integrations as part of ongoing maintenance.

(04)
What if my site wasn't built by Activeset originally?

No problem. We maintain Webflow sites regardless of who built them originally. We'll start with a comprehensive audit to understand your site's structure and identify any immediate issues that need attention.

(05)
How do you communicate what maintenance work you're doing?

We provide detailed monthly reports showing all work completed, performance metrics, and recommendations. Enterprise clients get dedicated Slack channels for real-time communication and updates.

(06)
Can I change or cancel my maintenance plan?

Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your maintenance plan at any time with 30 days notice. We want you to have the right level of support for your current needs.

(07)
Do you provide emergency support outside business hours?

Enterprise plan clients receive emergency support for critical issues that impact business operations. Growth and Essential plans receive next-business-day support for urgent issues.

(08)
What happens if you miss something or make a mistake?

We stand behind our work with a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If we miss something or make an error, we'll fix it immediately at no additional cost and review our processes to prevent similar issues.

(09)
How is this different from hiring an in-house person for website maintenance?

Our maintenance team includes specialists in performance optimization, security, SEO, and advanced Webflow development. You get expert-level knowledge across all areas for less than the cost of one full-time employee.
