## Content Guidelines
- **Word count:** 2,500-4,000 words
- **Rich elements:** Minimum 8 different types per post (TOC, stats, comparison, steps, features, callouts, FAQ, CTA)
- **Statistics:** Real, sourced data every 150-200 words
- **AEO optimization:** Question-format H2s, direct 40-60 word answers, FAQ schema
- **Schema:** FAQPage + Article + Organization + Person (JSON-LD)
- **Internal links:** 2-3 per post pointing to ActiveSet service pages
- **Refresh schedule:** Quarterly updates to maintain AI citations

## Brand Voice
- Professional but approachable
- Senior developer explaining to smart marketing director
- No fluff, no filler paragraphs
- Acknowledge limitations honestly (builds trust)
- Lead with data, not opinions
